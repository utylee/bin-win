New CF springs in the works for Kossel. I put cf springs from tape measures on Kossel a while back to balance out a suspended extruder. I think the force is OK but the springs are so heavy the inertia of spinning them quickly was dragging down the effector. These are extracted from badge holders don't know why they're so expensive. Do love cf springs though because you can easily adjust the force by making the wheel bigger. These will be super light and fast. Also instead of feeding them through a simple hole in the carriages I rigged up some tiny rc bearings.﻿
