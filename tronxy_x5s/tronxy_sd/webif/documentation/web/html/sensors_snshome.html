Expanding on its more than 30 year sensing history, NXP has unveiled a new era of sensing with the Sensing Solutions portfolio. This new brand of sensors is designed with the right combination of intelligent integration, logic and customizable software on the platform to deliver smarter, more differentiated applications.

Pressure Sensors
  • Wide variety of pressure ranges, diverse packaging and porting options.
  • TPMS, engine management, medical equipment, breathing apparatus, and industrial applications
Gyroscopes
  • Best in class power performance
  • Complete sensor fusion enablement suite
6-Axis Sensors
  • High accuracy accelerometer with magnetometer
  • Downloadable eCompass software
Touch Sensing
  • Enables autonomous high-precision sensing solutions
  • MEMS with integrated 32-bit MCU for intelligent motion, sensor hub and sensor data management
Magnetometers
  • High accuracy accelerometer with magnetometer
  • Downloadable eCompass software
Capacitive Sensors
  • Detects capacitance changes on a remote sensing plate
  • Proximity operation possible, no contact required
  • Changes in the static capacitance are automatically compensated through continuous auto-calibration
Intelligent Sensors
  • Enables autonomous high-precision sensing solutions
  • MEMS with integrated 32-bit MCU for intelligent motion, sensor hub and sensor data management
Angular Sensors
  • Extended temperature range
  • Accurate and reliable
  • Mechanical robustness, contactless principle
  • Wear-free operation
  • Accuracy independent of mechanical tolerances, magnetic drift with temperature or during life time
Temperature Sensors
  • Accurate and reliable
  • Long term stability
  • Positive temperature coefficient
  • Fail-safe behaviour
I²C Temperature / Voltage Monitors
  • High accuracy accelerometer with magnetometer
  • Downloadable eCompass software
Rotational Sensors

  • Wide air gap between sensor and target
  • Large measuring distance / air gap up to 2.9mm

  • High-frequency: 25 kHz
  • Wide frequency range
