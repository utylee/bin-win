We are currently in a state of transition as we merge the Freescale Connect Partner Program and the NXP Partner Program. At this time we are not accepting new enrollments or portal access to existing members. All Freescale Connect Partners are currently listed in the “Partner Directory” and NXP Partners can be found through the links on the “Overview tab.”

The NXP Partner Program is your essential source for embedded designs based on NXP solutions.
The program comprises a global network of independent engineering companies that offer the vital tools, software, technology, engineering services and training to speed your design. From reference boards to optimized software, the NXP Partner program provides a powerful and comprehensive ecosystem that partners with you in making the world a smarter, more connected place.

Premier partners collaborate closely with NXP to enrich the enablement and implementation of total system solutions.

Proven partners offer the knowledge and experience to round out the enablement and implementation of total system solutions.

NXP partners complete the ecosystem surrounding NXP technologies.

The NXP Partner Program is currently under construction for system updates and enhancements. At this time we will not be accepting any new enrollments. We will re-launch the program in early 2016. Thank you for your support.

The NXP Partner Program has three tiers. Premier and Proven are by invitation only. The third tier, Partner, is by application.

Benefits

  • Listing in the NXP partner database
  • Access to advanced roadmap and product information
  • Use of NXP Partner Program co-branding assets
  • Invitations to regional NXP events
  • Discounts on select development tools
  • All profiles now available in English and Simplified Chinese

Requirements

  • Provide and maintain company information including address and contact information, countries and languages supported, company description, number of engineers, and technology focus.
  • Provide and maintain accurate information regarding product and services related to NXP solutions.
  • Provide company logo for use on NXP.com and in program materials.
  • Be currently shipping NXP-based products or providing engineering services around NXP technologies.
  • Pay an annual $99 administration fee.
  • Abide by all program terms & conditions.
