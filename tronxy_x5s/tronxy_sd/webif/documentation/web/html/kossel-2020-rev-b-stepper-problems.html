Kossel 2020 Rev B Stepper Problems
taylorr 06 May 2016 03:30

Hey guys,

I am having trouble with my NEMA 17 axis steppers. I am working on a Folger Tech Kossel 2020 Rev B. After struggling with wiring for a bit I have gotten to where all three steppers are engaged (holding torque) when I try to move an axis in pronterface. However, regardless of which axis I move, all of the axes move strangely. Moving in 1mm steps, each stepper will move its carriage down for 5-6 steps, then up for 1, and repeat. This also happens regardless of the axis direction that I use in pronterface.

Trying to home any axis makes all three axes jog around for a bit (rather loudly) for about two seconds then they all stop. I have used M119 to check my endstops, they are all working correctly. The extruder stepper works just fine.

Does anyone have any idea what could be the cause?

On a side note, may/may not be related, I'm getting no response from the hot end heater, bed heater or from the 12v fans. I have flashed the most current firmware.bin and I'm using a sample config file for linear delta machines found on smoothiewareDelta

Any help would be greatly appreciated.
