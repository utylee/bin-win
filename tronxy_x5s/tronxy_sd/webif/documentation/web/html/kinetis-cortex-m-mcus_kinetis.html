Kinetis K Series
  • Broad scalability and compatibility
  • 50-180 MHz
  • 32 KB-2 MB Flash
  • Up to 256 KB SRAM
  • Floating-point unit, security, analog, and serial interfaces
Kinetis V Series
  • High-speed, high-precision analog and timing peripherals
  • 75-220 MHz performance
  • 16KB-1MB Flash
  • Up to 256 KB SRAM
  • Solutions optimized for real time computation, including math accelerators
Kinetis M Series
  • Metering and measurement applications
  • 50 MHz performance
  • 32-128 KB Flash
  • Up to 32 KB RAM
  • High accuracy Sigma Delta analog front end, security, and serial interfaces
Kinetis W Series
  • Integrated with sub-1 GHz and 2.4 GHz RF transceivers
  • 48-50 MHz performance
  • 128-512 KB Flash
  • Up to 64 KB SRAM
  • Optimized for embedded wireless solutions
Kinetis MINI MCUs
  • Feature-rich ARM
    Cortex-M4 and M0+    -based MCUs
  • Designed in miniature chip-scale packages (CSPs)
  • Optimized for space-constrained applications

  • Automotive grade
    qualification

  • 8-128 KB Flash
  • Up to 16 KB RAM

Kinetis E Series
  • Enhanced ESD/EMC performance
  • Up to 48 MHz performance
  • 8-128 KB Flash
  • Up to 16 KB RAM
