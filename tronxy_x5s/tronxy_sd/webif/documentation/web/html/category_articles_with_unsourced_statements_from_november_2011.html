This category combines all articles with unsourced statements from November 2011 (2011-11) to enable us to work through the backlog more systematically. It is a member of Category:Articles with unsourced statements.
To add an article to this category add {{Citation needed|date=November 2011}} to the article. If you omit the date a bot will add it for you at some point.
