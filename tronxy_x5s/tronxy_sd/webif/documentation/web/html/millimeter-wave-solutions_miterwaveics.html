Millimeter-Wave Solutions

Our 77 GHz silicon germanium (SiGe) automotive radar solutions advance automotive safety by enabling vehicles to sense potential crash situations. These radar solutions enable long- and mid-range functionality, allowing automotive systems to monitor the environment around the vehicle to help prevent crashes. These radar solutions are based on multichannel receivers and transmitters that allow high-level integration and complex signal generation and processing suitable for advanced automotive safety systems.
