NXP Technology Days

Better insight, Better tools, Better training

NXP Technology Days provide tailored live training to embedded engineers on a broad range of solutions. Available in convenient locations globally, with more than 250 seminars offered globally, you can find the right training you need in a city near you.

Types of Seminars

Solutions Seminars (SS)

May include a variety of lectures and hands-on training, as well as a Technology Lab exhibiting our latest solutions and and those from our ecosystem partners.

Content Specific (COS)

Our instructor-led training courses created to enable embedded engineers to accelerate the development of innovative end–products utilizing NXP technologies.

Course Materials

Join and find technical sessions and support material delivered at NXP Training Community
