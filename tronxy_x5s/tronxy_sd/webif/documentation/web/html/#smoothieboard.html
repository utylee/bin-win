3D Printing

Brad Smith

Custom-built & Reprap  -  2015-01-25
 
I designed and made a mount for my #smoothieboard thisafternoon.   It is designed to be bolted in the 100mm void  between the floor and base plates of my Delta printer.   The fans are twin 24vdc 40mm (I need to purchase another)  keeping the electronics nice and cool.  Lit attaches to the facia with 2 x M3 cap screws and the legs at the opposite end are mounted the same way.   Then the whole assemble is bolted to the plates with M4 cap screws.    Nice to get it mounted and safe. 

It was printed on my UpMini using a 0.3 later height and normal speed and was at the limits of it's build volume.  I am still waiting for parts for the heater bed on my delta which will hopefully arrive next week, then i can start building some big stuff  ﻿

Felix 3D Printer Mods

Alex Skoruppa

Partagé en mode public  -  2015-09-14
 
I found the time for a photo shooting of my #FrankensteinFelix.

Mods so far:
- Modified #E3Dv6 print head by +Michel Satoer with inductive sensor
- PEI coated aluminum printed with high power 180W Kapton heater
- Printbed base from a #Prusa_i3 like machine
- #BondtechExtruder by +Martin Bondéus
- #SmoothieBoard 4C controller
- #Bicker BEO-3624 fanless PSU
- M12 sensor/actuator cable for easy separation of printhead and electronics compartment
- Custom designed guide for filament tube and M12 cable
- 6x RADDS Hall-E Endstops on Min&Max of all axes
- Custom designed magnet holders that trigger the end stops. Magnets used are 10x10x5mm neodym magnets from Amazon.
- Astrosyn dampeners on the X/Y/Z steppers
- Custom designed enclosures for all electronic parts
- Custom designed feet that are filled with #TaicaGel pads

Of course you can get all the designs from my GitHub repo. Have a look for the folders starting with Felix_:
https://github.com/grainiac/3D_Printing﻿
