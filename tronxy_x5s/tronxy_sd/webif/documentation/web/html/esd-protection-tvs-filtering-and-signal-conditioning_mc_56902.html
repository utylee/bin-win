USB Type-C protection family

Containing a lot of power and characterized by very fast rise-times, electrostatic discharge (ESD) pulses are a major cause of damage and malfunction in electronic equipment. We offer an extensive range of USB protection devices that deliver outstanding protection against ESD and other voltage-induced transient pulses.
