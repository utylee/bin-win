Automotive MOSFETs

NXP's advanced MOSFET solutions deliver the flexibility you need in today's market. Choose from a wide range of general purpose and dedicated automotive power MOSFET solutions. For RF applications, we also offer a world-leading range of LDMOS power transistors as well as field-proven small-signal FETs.
High Side Switches
  • 1+ channels of low Rds(ON) MOSFET output
  • Control resistive and inductive load high-side switching
  • N-channel MOSFET with extremely low on-resistance
Low Side Switches
  • 1+ channels of low Rds(ON) MOSFET output
  • Control resistive and inductive load switching to ground
Pre-Drivers
  • Integrated charge pump, gate drive and protection
  • Drive external MOSFET switches
Squib Drivers
  • Squib diagnostic and deployment interfaces for automotive air bag modules

RF Small-Signal MOSFETs
