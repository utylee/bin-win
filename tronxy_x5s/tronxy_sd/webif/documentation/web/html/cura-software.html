 Release notes

Cura, the world’s
most advanced
3D printer software

Intuitive, intelligent and engineered to either hold your hand or give you complete freedom.

World-class
software for free

Developed through open source collaboration, Cura is not only the most advanced 3D software around – it’s free too.

Making the best
even better

Our dedicated software team is constantly refining Cura adding new features for the enjoyment of all users.

Fast draft or
high-res mode

There are times you want perfection, other times you just need a quick preview. Cura puts you in the driving seat.

Reliable and open source

Built on the robust Uranium framework, Cura’s incredibly reliable and flexible, allowing our dedicated software team to easily add new features. There’s even a certified global support team on hand to answer all your questions.

Sharing Cura with the industry

Because Cura is open source, everyone gets to enjoy it. In fact, thanks to its robust reliability, several ten thousands of 3D printers use Cura – helping the 3D world to grow. See, sharing is good.

A global R&D team

As Cura’s open source, our community of global experts are able to create their own enhancements. These plugins are then free for everyone else to enjoy. Together we’re stronger.
